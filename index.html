The Latest Podcasts

Hosted by Dante and Sean (@SeanCarrollNBA), The Deep Two is a weekly NBA podcast that goes live weekly to talk about all things NBA-related from opening night all the way to the offseason. We hope you enjoy!

Ep. 174 | Trade talks hit the bain-marie, who blinks first among the “tanking” teams?

The early-season trade talks have begun as the LA Clippers look at Myles Turner, the Boston Celtics look at Jakob Poeltl and much more. We also discuss the second TDT Bowl of the 2022-23 season, looking at what went wrong for the Golden State Warriors against the Phoenix Suns before having a chat about the tanking teams, looking at if some of them have been winning too many games to be in the Victor Wembanyama race.

Ep. 173 | Cam Johnson's injury, Anthony Davis trades and Golden State growing pains

In this week's episode, we tackle the big news of the week including (but not limited to) Cam Johnson's torn meniscus and what it means for the Phoenix Suns, the potential for an Anthony Davis trade, if Steve Clifford is long for the Charlotte Hornets and what role Jonothan Isaac might play for the Orlando Magic if he returns. We wrap up with a discussion about the Golden State Warriors, dissecting their early-season struggles.

Ep. 172 | Kyrie Irving, Ime Udoka and the Brooklyn Nets

The Brooklyn Nets have come to the verge of implosion once again, and we break down Kyrie Irving’s disappointing antisemitism saga and the Nets’ shock move to install suspended Boston Celtics coach Ime Udoka after firing Steve Nash. Plus, we talk about what this team might actually look like on the court after the scandals of the last week.

Ep. 171 | Small sample size theatre, Russell Westbrook trade rumours

The NBA is BACK (in full) and it’s a great time to be a basketball fan. This week we’re diving into all the excitement of the new season, from the Minnesota Timberwolves’ Twin Towers experiment to the Golden State Warriors’ continued excellence. We also chat Detroit Pistons and Indiana Pacers and check in on the Denver Nuggets, before updating the yucky Russell Westbrook situation in LA.

Ep. 170 | NBA Opening Night 2022-23, no extension for Cam Johnson?

NBA OPENING NIGHT IS HERE - join us as we dive into everything opening night threw at us, including a lacklustre LA Lakers performance and an intriguing chess match between the Philadelphia 76ers and Boston Celtics. Then, we discuss Cam Johnson’s extension that never was and what it means for the Phoenix Suns moving forward.

About

The Deep Two is an Australian-based podcast that talks about all things basketball. Established in 2019, hosts Dante and Sean run through everything happening in the NBA as well as run the accompanying blog. If you’d like to contribute, got a question you want us to answer on-air or just say hello, you can find us on social media or at deeptwopodcast@gmail.com

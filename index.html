The Latest Podcasts

Hosted by Dante and Sean (@SeanCarrollNBA), The Deep Two is a weekly NBA podcast that goes live weekly to talk about all things NBA-related from opening night all the way to the offseason. We hope you enjoy!

Ep. 181 | 2022-23 Midseason Awards with Marco Holden Jeffery, JVG NBA Tribute Show

We've passed the official midway point of the 2022-23 season and Marco jumps on the pod to run through the awards races. We discuss the regulars, like MVP, DPoY, Rookie of the Year as well as a few special awards we've added in like Most Disappointing Team and NOT Executive of the Year.

Ep. 180 | Settling the Caris LeVert debate, Jon Isaac sighting with Lukas Petridis, JVG NBA Tribute Show

Caris LeVert is a very interesting player and after inviting on Lukas Petridis of The JVG NBA Tribute Show, we hash out an old debate on his trade value around the league. From there, we touch on how good the 2020 Bubble was before looking at the news of the week including Jon Isaac’s return to the court, more potential Phoenix trade partners and how the Golden State Warriors can fix their roster issues.

Ep. 179 | Major names decline extensions, what it means for the 2023 Trade Deadline

In this week's episode of The Deep Two NBA Podcast, we take a look at the recent flurry of non-extensions that players across the league haven't signed including Myles Turner, Fred VanVleet and Jordan Clarkson. From there, we take a look at what it'll mean for their trade markets and what the Play In Tournament has done for the trade market as a whole.

Ep. 178 | Luka Doncic's historic night, Christmas news

This week we’re pushing through the Christmas haze to break down Luka Doncic’s historic performance against the New York Knicks and Devin Booker’s pesky injury situation and what it means for the Phoenix Suns. Plus, we’ll touch on the Chicago Bulls’ potential to blow it up and the latest chapter in the ugly Miles Bridges saga with the Charlotte Hornets.

Ep. 177 | Are the Chicago Bulls f**ked? + news

The Chicago Bulls are struggling in this young NBA season and currently five games under .500, the rumours of tearing it all down and rebuilding have started. We look at how they might tear it down or if it's worth doubling down on this current core and looking to bolster the roster for a potential playoff push. From there, we touch on the news of the week including possible Eric Gordon trades, the new NBA award names, smaller trade rumours and the future of Bradley beal after his recent comments about free agency.

About

The Deep Two is an Australian-based podcast that talks about all things basketball. Established in 2019, hosts Dante and Sean run through everything happening in the NBA as well as run the accompanying blog. If you’d like to contribute, got a question you want us to answer on-air or just say hello, you can find us on social media or at deeptwopodcast@gmail.com
